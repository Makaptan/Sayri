Jing Lavilles de Egurrola

The Cebu Highlands Trail

A personal and pioneering account of the creation and first thruhike of the Cebu Highlands Trail—a 408-kilometer path stretching from Santander to Daanbantayan. Blending grit, vision, and community spirit, this story chronicles how one hiker turned a solitary dream into a legacy trail that reconnects people with the heart of Cebu’s wild interior. More than a physical journey, it’s a cultural and spiritual pilgrimage, stitched together by faith, generosity, and a deep love for the land.
